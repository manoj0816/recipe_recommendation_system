Iced Coffee

Total Time: 25 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • Iced Coffee
  • Ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Brew coffee and let it cool.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the cooled coffee over the ice.
  4. Add milk or cream and sugar, then stir well.

Matcha Latte

Total Time: 3 hours(including chilling) | Servings:4 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp matcha powder
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1-2 tsp honey or sugar

Procedure:

  1. Whisk matcha powder with hot water until smooth.
  2. Heat milk and froth it using a frother.
  3. Pour the frothed milk into the matcha mixture.
  4. Sweeten if desired and serve warm.

Chai Tea

Total Time: 6 hours(including chilling) | Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 black tea bag
  • 1 tsp chai spices (cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, cloves)
  • 1-2 tsp sugar

Procedure:

  1. Boil water and add tea bag and spices.
  2. Simmer for 5 minutes, then add milk and sugar.
  3. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  4. Strain and serve hot

Bubble Tea (Boba Tea)

Total Time: 2 hours(including cooling,Preparation: 15 minutes, Baking: 50 minutes, Cooling & Chilling: 1 hour ) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup tapioca pearls
  • 1 cup brewed black tea
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Ice cubes

Procedure:

  1. Boil tapioca pearls until soft, then drain.
  2. Brew black tea and let it cool.
  3. Mix tea, milk, and sugar.
  4. Add ice and tapioca pearls, then serve.

Cold Brew Coffee

Total Time: 25 minutes (Preparation: 10 minutes, Baking: 12–15 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup ground coffee
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or sweetener (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Mix coffee with cold water and let it steep for 12-24 hours.
  2. Strain and serve over ice with milk or sweetener.

Hot Chocolate

Total Time: 4 hours (including chilling,Preparation: 10 minutes, Cooking: 10 minutes, Chilling: 3–4 hours)| Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Heat milk and mix in cocoa powder and sugar.
  2. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  3. Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream.

  • Ice cubes

Procedure:

  1. Mix ginger juice, lemon juice, and honey in a glass.
  2. Add ice cubes and pour soda water over it.
  3. Stir well and serve chilled.

Virgin Mojito

Total Time: 45 hours (Preparation: 15 minutes, Baking: 25 minutes, Cooling: 5 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 lime (sliced)
  • 1 cup soda water
  • Ice cubes

Procedure:

  1. Muddle mint leaves, sugar, and lime slices in a glass.
  2. Add ice cubes and pour soda water over it.
  3. Stir and serve chilled.

Piña Colada (Non-Alcoholic)

Total Time: 4 hours | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup crushed ice
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • Pineapple slice for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Blend pineapple juice, coconut milk, and crushed ice until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with a pineapple slice.
  3. Serve chilled.

Blue Lagoon (Mocktail Version)

Total Time: 2.5 hours (Preparation: 20 minutes, Baking: 1.5 hours, Cooling: 30 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup lemon-lime soda
  • 1 tbsp blue curaçao syrup (non-alcoholic)
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slice for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add blue curaçao syrup and top with lemon-lime soda.
  3. Stir and garnish with a lemon slice.
