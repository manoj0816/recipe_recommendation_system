Chocolate Lava Cake

Total Time: 25 minutes | Servings: 4

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Grease four ramekins with butter and dust with flour.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate, mix well.
  3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Mix everything together and pour into ramekins.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes. Let cool for 1 min and serve warm.

Classic Tiramisu

Total Time: 4 hours | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 8 oz (227g) mascarpone cheese
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup brewed espresso (cooled)
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfinger biscuits
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder (for dusting)

Procedure:

  1. Prepare Cream: Beat heavy cream, sugar, vanilla extract, and mascarpone cheese until smooth and creamy.
  2. Prepare Coffee Mixture: In a bowl, mix espresso with coffee liqueur.
  3. Dip & Layer: Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture for about 2 seconds (don’t soak too much). Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in a dish.
  4. Add Cream: Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat another layer with soaked ladyfingers and the remaining cream.
  5. Chill: Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (preferably overnight).
  6. Add Cream: Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat another layer with soaked ladyfingers and the remaining cream.

Vanilla Panna Cotta

Total Time: 3 hours(including chilling) | Servings:4 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 packet (2 ½ tsp) gelatin
  • 2 tbsp cold water

Procedure:

  1. Prepare Gelatin: In a small bowl, sprinkle gelatin over cold water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat Cream: In a saucepan, heat heavy cream, milk, and sugar over medium heat until warm but not boiling. Stir until sugar dissolves.
  3. Add Gelatin: Remove from heat and add the bloomed gelatin. Stir until completely dissolved.
  4. Add Vanilla & Pour: Mix in vanilla extract, then pour the mixture into ramekins or serving glasses.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 3 hours until set.
  6. Serve: Optionally, top with fresh berries or caramel sauce before serving.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Total Time: 6 hours(including chilling) | Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups crushed graham crackers
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 tbsp sugar

For The Filling:

  • 16 oz (454g) cream cheese (room temperature)
  • >½ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries (blended into a puree)

Procedure:

  1. Prepare Crust: Mix crushed graham crackers, melted butter, and sugar. Press the mixture into the base of a springform pan. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. Make Filling: Beat cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla until smooth. Add heavy whipping cream and beat until fluffy. Mix in strawberry puree.
  3. Assemble & Chill: Pour the filling over the crust and smooth the top. Cover and refrigerate for at least 5-6 hours (or overnight).
  4. Garnish & Serve: Top with fresh strawberries before serving.

Classic Brownies

Total Time: 35 minutes | Servings: 12

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp baking powder

Procedure:

  1. Preheat Oven: Set oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Melt Butter & Mix: Melt butter, then mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add Dry Ingredients: Sift in flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Stir until combined.
  4. Bake: Pour batter into the pan and bake for 20-25 minutes. A toothpick inserted should come out with a few moist crumbs.
  5. Cool & Serve: Let it cool before slicing. Enjoy warm or chilled.

Red Velvet Cake

Total Time: 1 hours 30 minutes(Preparation: 30 minutes, Baking: 30 minutes, Cooling & Frosting: 30 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups (360ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk, room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) red food coloring
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar

For The Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 8 oz (225g) cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cups (480g) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a bowl, whisk flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. In another bowl, whisk vegetable oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla extract, and vinegar.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet ingredients, mixing until smooth.
  5. Divide batter evenly between cake pans.
    Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
    Let cakes cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Make the frosting:Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth.
    Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla, beating until fluffy.
  7. Spread frosting between layers, on top, and around the cake.
  8. Chill for 30 minutes before serving.

Crème Brûlée

Total Time: 2 hours(including cooling,Preparation: 15 minutes, Baking: 50 minutes, Cooling & Chilling: 1 hour ) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (480ml) heavy cream
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar (plus extra for caramelizing)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C). Place four ramekins in a baking dish.
  2. Heat cream over medium heat until hot but not boiling.
  3. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale.
  4. Gradually add warm cream while whisking continuously.
  5. Stir in vanilla extract.
  6. Strain mixture into ramekins.
  7. Pour hot water into the baking dish (halfway up the ramekins).
  8. Bake for 45–50 minutes until slightly jiggly in the center.
  9. Cool at room temperature, then refrigerate for 1 hour.
  10. Sprinkle a thin layer of sugar on top.
  11. Use a kitchen torch to caramelize until golden brown.
  12. Let sit for a minute before serving.

Molten Lava Cake

Total Time: 25 minutes (Preparation: 10 minutes, Baking: 12–15 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 4 oz (115g) dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons (15g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour four ramekins.
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together over a double boiler or in the microwave. Stir until smooth.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until pale and thick.
  4. Stir in melted chocolate mixture, then add flour, salt, and vanilla.
  5. Divide batter into ramekins.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes until edges are set but centers are soft.
  7. Let sit for 1 minute, then invert onto plates. Serve warm with ice cream.

Panna Cotta

Total Time: 4 hours (including chilling,Preparation: 10 minutes, Cooking: 10 minutes, Chilling: 3–4 hours)| Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (480ml) heavy cream
  • ½ cup (120ml) whole milk
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ teaspoons (7g) gelatin powder
  • 3 tablespoons (45ml) cold water

Procedure:

  1. In a small bowl, sprinkle gelatin over cold water and let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a saucepan, heat cream, milk, and sugar over medium heat until hot but not boiling
  3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla
  4. Add bloomed gelatin to the hot mixture, stirring until fully dissolved.
  5. Pour mixture into ramekins or molds.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 3–4 hours until set.
  7. Run a knife around the edges and invert onto plates. Serve with fruit or sauce.

Éclairs

Total Time: 2 hours (Preparation: 45 minutes, Baking: 30 minutes, Filling & Cooling: 45 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Choux Pastry:

  • 1 cup (240ml) water
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For the Pastry Cream (Filling):

  • 2 cups (480ml) whole milk
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup (30g) cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) unsalted butter

For the Chocolate Glaze:

  • 4 oz (115g) dark chocolate, chopped
  • ½ cup (120ml) heavy cream

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, heat water, butter, and salt until boiling.
  3. Add flour and stir continuously until dough pulls away from the sides.
  4. Let cool for 5 minutes, then beat in eggs one at a time.
  5. Pipe dough onto a baking sheet in 4-inch logs.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden and puffed. Cool completely.
  7. Heat milk over medium heat.
  8. Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch in a bowl.
  9. Slowly add warm milk, whisking constantly.
  10. Return to heat, stirring until thick.
  11. Remove from heat, add vanilla and butter. Let cool.
  12. Slice éclairs in half and pipe pastry cream inside.
  13. Heat cream and pour over chopped chocolate. Stir until smooth.
  14. Dip the tops of éclairs in chocolate glaze.
  15. Let set before serving.

Lemon Tart

Total Time: 2 hours (Preparation: 30 minutes, Baking: 30 minutes, Cooling: 1 hour) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Tart Crust:

  • 1 ¼ cups (160g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon cold water

For the Lemon Filling:

  • 3 large eggs
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (120ml) fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • ½ cup (120ml) heavy cream

Procedure:

  1. Blend flour, butter, and sugar in a food processor.
  2. Add egg yolk and water, mixing until dough forms.
  3. Chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out and fit into a tart pan.
  5. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Whisk eggs, sugar, lemon juice, and zest.
  7. Stir in heavy cream.
  8. Pour filling into the crust.
  9. Bake for 20–25 minutes until set.
  10. Cool and refrigerate before serving.

Strawberry Shortcake

Total Time:45 minutes (Preparation: 20 minutes, Baking: 20 minutes, Assembling: 5 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Shortcake:

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup (67g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, cubed
  • ¾ cup (180ml) heavy cream

For the Filling:

  • 2 cups (300g) fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) whipped cream

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cut in butter until crumbly.
  4. Stir in cream, forming a dough.
  5. Roll out and cut into rounds.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes.
  7. Toss strawberries with sugar. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  8. Slice shortcakes in half.
  9. Layer with strawberries and whipped cream.

Macarons

Total Time: 2.5 hours (Preparation: 30 minutes, Resting: 1 hour, Baking: 15 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Macaron Shells:

  • 1 cup (100g) almond flour
  • 1 cup (125g) powdered sugar
  • 2 large egg whites
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • Food coloring (optional)

For the Filling (Buttercream):

  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (125g) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Procedure:

  1. Sift almond flour and powdered sugar.
  2. Whisk egg whites until foamy, then add sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  3. Fold in dry ingredients gently.
  4. Pipe small circles onto a baking sheet.
  5. Let sit for 1 hour.
  6. Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C).
  7. Bake for 12–15 minutes. Let cool.
  8. Beat butter, sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  9. Pipe filling onto one macaron shell and top with another.

Baklava

Total Time: 3 hours (Preparation: 30 minutes, Baking: 1 hour, Cooling: 1.5 hours) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 package (16 oz) phyllo dough
  • 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 cups (250g) chopped walnuts or pistachios
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

For the Syrup:

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • ½ cup (120ml) honey
  • ½ cup (120ml) water
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Procedure:

  1. Mix nuts and cinnamon.
  2. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  3. Brush a baking dish with butter.
  4. Layer phyllo sheets, brushing each with butter.
  5. Add a layer of nuts after every few sheets.
  6. Continue layering, finishing with phyllo.
  7. Cut into diamond shapes.
  8. Bake for 50–60 minutes.
  9. Simmer sugar, honey, water, and lemon juice for 10 minutes.
  10. Pour syrup over hot baklava. Let cool before serving.

Black Forest Cake

Total Time: 2.5 hours (Preparation: 45 minutes, Baking: 35 minutes, Cooling & Assembly: 1 hour) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Chocolate Cake:

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¾ cups (350g) granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup (75g) unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
  • ½ cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240ml) boiling water

For the Filling & Decoration:

  • 2 cups (480ml) heavy cream
  • ¼ cup (30g) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups (250g) pitted cherries (fresh or canned)
  • ¼ cup (60ml) cherry liqueur (optional)
  • 1 cup (100g) chocolate shavings

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Stir in boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Divide into pans and bake for 30–35 minutes. Cool completely.
  6. Beat heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla until stiff peaks form.
  7. Drain cherries and mix with cherry liqueur if using.
  8. Slice cakes in half horizontally.
  9. Layer with whipped cream and cherries.
  10. Frost the outside with whipped cream and decorate with chocolate shavings and cherries.

Cannoli

Total Time: 2 hours (Preparation: 45 minutes, Frying: 15 minutes, Cooling & Filling: 1 hour) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Shells:

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup (120ml) white wine or water
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For the Ricotta Filling:

  • 2 cups (480g) ricotta cheese, drained
  • ¾ cup (90g) powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ cup (50g) mini chocolate chips

Procedure:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt. Cut in butter.
  2. Add egg and wine/water, kneading into a dough.
  3. Wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll dough thin, cut circles, and wrap around cannoli molds.
  5. Fry in hot oil (375°F/190°C) until golden. Drain and cool.
  6. Mix ricotta, powdered sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Pipe filling into cooled shells. Dust with powdered sugar before serving.

Cupcakes

Total Time: 45 hours (Preparation: 15 minutes, Baking: 20 minutes, Cooling & Frosting: 10 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup (120ml) milk

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Beat butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients alternately with milk.
  5. Fill liners ¾ full and bake for 18–20 minutes. Cool before frosting.
  6. Use buttercream or cream cheese frosting.

Banana Pudding

Total Time: 4 hours (Preparation: 20 minutes, Chilling: 3–4 hours) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups (720ml) whole milk
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup (30g) cornstarch
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 2 cups (250g) vanilla wafers
  • 2 cups (480ml) whipped cream

Procedure:

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan. Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch.
  2. Slowly add warm milk, whisking constantly.
  3. Return to heat, stir until thick, then add vanilla.
  4. Layer vanilla wafers, banana slices, and custard. Repeat layers.
  5. Top with whipped cream and chill for at least 3 hours.

Profiteroles

  • 1 cup (240ml) water

  • 2 cups (480ml) heavy cream
  • ¼ cup (30g) powdered sugar

For the Chocolate Sauce:

Carrot Cake

For the Cake:

  • 2 cups (250g) all-purpose flour

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar

For the Frosting:

Fruit Tart

Total Time: 4 hours | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Crust:

  • 1 ¼ cups (160g) all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • ¼ cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon cold water

For the Pastry Cream:

  • 2 cups (480ml) whole milk
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup (30g) cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) unsalted butter

For Topping:

    - 2 cups mixed fresh fruits (strawberries, kiwi, blueberries, etc.) - ¼ cup (60ml) apricot jam (for glaze)

  1. Blend flour, butter, and sugar in a food processor. Add egg yolk and water.
  2. Chill for 30 minutes, then roll out and fit into a tart pan.

  4. Heat milk. Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch in a bowl.
  5. Slowly add warm milk, whisking constantly.
  6. Return to heat, stir until thick, then add vanilla and butter.
  7. Spread pastry cream in cooled crust.
  8. Arrange fresh fruit on top. Brush with warm apricot jam for shine.

Mango Sticky Rice

Total Time: 4 hours | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (200g) glutinous rice (sticky rice)
  • 1 ½ cups (360ml) coconut milk
  • ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Soak rice in water for 1 hour, then steam for 25–30 minutes until tender.
  2. Heat coconut milk, sugar, and salt until dissolved.

  4. Plate sticky rice with mango slices. Drizzle extra coconut milk and sprinkle sesame seeds.

Pavlova

Total Time: 2.5 hours (Preparation: 20 minutes, Baking: 1.5 hours, Cooling: 30 minutes) | Servings: 6

Ingredients:

For the Meringue:

  • 4 large egg whites
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar or lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For Topping:

  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream, whipped
  • 1 cup fresh fruits (strawberries, kiwi, passion fruit)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 250°F (120°C).
  2. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
  3. Fold in vinegar, cornstarch, and vanilla.
  4. Shape meringue into a round disc on a lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 1.5 hours, then turn off the oven and let cool inside.
  6. Top with whipped cream and fresh fruits.
