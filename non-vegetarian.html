Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken 🍗🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 300g Chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 cup Cashew Paste
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tbsp Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • In a bowl, marinate the chicken with yogurt, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, and salt. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  • Heat oil and butter in a pan on medium flame.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add more butter and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating.
  • Add cashew paste and mix well for a rich texture.
  • Return the cooked chicken to the pan and stir well.
  • Add water and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Stir in fresh cream for a creamy consistency.
  • Crush kasuri methi between your palms and add to the gravy for an enhanced flavor.
  • Add sugar (optional) and mix everything well. Simmer for another 2 minutes.
  • Turn off the heat and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or basmati rice.

Enjoy your rich and creamy Butter Chicken at home! 😋🔥

Chicken Biryani

🍗 Chicken Biryani 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups Basmati Rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 300g Chicken (bone-in or boneless, cut into pieces)
  • 1 Onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Biryani Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2-3 Cloves
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 Green Cardamoms
  • 1 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Mint Leaves (chopped)
  • 2.5 Cups Water
  • Salt (to taste)

📌 Procedure

  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain before cooking.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a large pan or pressure cooker.
  • Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, and cardamoms. Sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Mix in turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, biryani masala, and salt.
  • Add chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes until they turn white.
  • Stir in the yogurt and mix well.
  • Pour in 2.5 cups of water and bring to a boil.
  • Add the soaked basmati rice and mix gently.
  • Sprinkle fresh mint and coriander leaves on top.
  • Cover and cook on low flame for 20 minutes (or 1 whistle in a pressure cooker).
  • Let it sit for 5 minutes before fluffing the biryani gently with a fork.
  • Serve hot with raita, salad, or pickle.

Enjoy your aromatic and delicious Chicken Biryani! 🍛🔥

Chicken Tikka Masala

🍗 Chicken Tikka Masala 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter

🍛 Masala Sauce

  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, coriander powder, lemon juice, and salt.
  • Add chicken pieces and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight for best results).
  • Heat oil or butter in a pan and grill the marinated chicken until fully cooked. Set aside.
  • In the same pan, heat butter and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  • Pour in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  • Add red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and cumin powder. Mix well.
  • Add water and bring to a boil.
  • Stir in heavy cream and mix until smooth.
  • Add the cooked chicken pieces and simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

Enjoy your rich and flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala! 🍛🔥

Chicken 65

🍗 Chicken 65 🔥

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken (boneless, cut into small pieces)
  • 2 tbsp Corn Flour
  • 1 tbsp All-Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 Egg (beaten)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil (for deep frying)
  • 10-12 Curry Leaves
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp Yogurt (optional, for extra flavor)

📌 Procedure

  • In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with corn flour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, lemon juice, egg, and salt.
  • Marinate for at least 30 minutes (for best flavor, marinate for 2 hours).
  • Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the marinated chicken pieces until golden brown and crispy. Drain excess oil and set aside.
  • In another pan, heat 1 tbsp oil and add curry leaves and green chilies.
  • Add the fried chicken pieces and sauté for a minute for extra flavor.
  • Optionally, mix in 1 tbsp yogurt for a slight tangy taste.
  • Serve hot with lemon wedges and onion slices.

Enjoy your crispy and spicy Chicken 65! 🍗🔥

Mutton Rogan Josh

🍖 Mutton Rogan Josh 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Mutton (bone-in pieces)
  • 2 Onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri Red Chili Powder (for color)
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Fennel Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Asafoetida (Hing)
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2-3 Cloves
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 Green Cardamoms
  • 1 Black Cardamom
  • 1 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • 2 cups Water
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Heat ghee or oil in a deep pan or pressure cooker.
  • Add bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, green and black cardamoms, and sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Add mutton pieces and sear until they turn brown.
  • Mix in turmeric powder, red chili powder, Kashmiri chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, fennel powder, and salt.
  • Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  • Whisk yogurt and add it slowly while stirring to prevent curdling.
  • Pour in 2 cups of water, cover, and cook on low flame for 1.5 to 2 hours (or 4-5 whistles in a pressure cooker) until the mutton is tender.
  • Add garam masala and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

Enjoy your rich and aromatic Mutton Rogan Josh! 🍖🔥

Keema Matar

🍖 Keema Matar (Minced Meat with Green Peas) 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Minced Mutton or Chicken
  • 1 cup Green Peas (Matar, fresh or frozen)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Fennel Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 2 Cloves
  • 1-inch Cinnamon Stick
  • 2 Green Cardamoms
  • 1 Black Cardamom
  • 1 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • 1 cup Water
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Heat ghee or oil in a deep pan.
  • Add bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, green and black cardamoms, and sauté for a few seconds.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.

Enjoy your delicious and flavorful Keema Matar! 🍖🔥

Lamb Seekh Kebab

🥩 Lamb Seekh Kebab 🔥

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Lamb Mince (finely ground)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped or grated)
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Mint Leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1-2 Green Chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Chaat Masala (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Ghee or Oil (for basting)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Wooden or Metal Skewers

📌 Procedure

  • In a large bowl, mix lamb mince with chopped onions, coriander, mint, and green chilies.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, black pepper, and salt.
  • Drizzle lemon juice and mix everything well to form a smooth kebab mixture.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to enhance flavors.
  • Preheat the grill or tandoor to medium-high heat.
  • Wet your hands, take a portion of the mixture, and shape it around a skewer, pressing gently to form an even kebab.
  • Repeat with the remaining mixture.
  • Grill the kebabs, turning occasionally, while basting with ghee or oil until golden brown and fully cooked (8-10 minutes).
  • Remove from skewers and serve hot with mint chutney, onion rings, and lemon wedges.

Enjoy your smoky and flavorful Lamb Seekh Kebabs! 🔥🥩

Fish Curry

🐟 Fish Curry 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Fish (Pomfret, Rohu, Tilapia, or any firm fish, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp Mustard Oil or Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Fenugreek Seeds (optional)
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Pulp or Lemon Juice
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk or Water (for a thinner curry)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Wash the fish pieces and marinate them with turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 15 minutes.
  • Heat mustard oil in a pan until it starts to smoke, then reduce the heat.
  • Fry the fish pieces lightly for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same oil, add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cooking until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add tomato puree, cumin powder, coriander powder, black pepper, and garam masala. Cook until oil separates.
  • Pour in coconut milk or water and bring to a boil.
  • Add the fried fish pieces and simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  • Mix in tamarind pulp or lemon juice for a tangy taste.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Enjoy your flavorful and aromatic Fish Curry with rice or naan! 🐟🔥

Prawn Masala

🍤 Prawn Masala 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp Oil (Coconut or Vegetable Oil)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Fennel Seeds (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk (optional, for a creamy texture)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Wash and pat dry the prawns, then marinate them with turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Set aside for 10 minutes.
  • Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and fennel seeds. Let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cooking until the raw smell disappears.
  • Add tomato puree, cumin powder, coriander powder, black pepper, and garam masala. Cook until oil separates.
  • Stir in the marinated prawns and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Pour in coconut milk (if using) and simmer for 2 minutes.
  • Drizzle lemon juice and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

Enjoy your spicy and flavorful Prawn Masala with rice or roti! 🍤🔥

Crab Masala

🦀 Crab Masala 🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g Crab (cleaned and cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp Oil (Coconut or Vegetable Oil)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 Green Chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper Powder
  • 1 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Fennel Seeds (optional)
  • 1/2 cup Coconut Milk (optional, for a creamy texture)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves (for garnish)

Masala Omelette

🍳 Masala Omelette 🌶️

🥘 Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/4 tsp Cumin Powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp Milk (optional, for a fluffier omelette)
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter

📌 Procedure

  • Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat well.
  • Add chopped onions, tomatoes, green chili, and coriander leaves.
  • Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, and salt.
  • Optionally, add milk for a softer texture and whisk until frothy.
  • Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  • Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread evenly.
  • Cook on low-medium flame until the bottom is golden brown.
  • Flip the omelette carefully and cook for another minute.
  • Once done, transfer to a plate and serve hot.

Enjoy your spicy and fluffy Masala Omelette with toast or roti! 🍳🔥

Egg Bhurji

🥚 Egg Bhurji 🌶️

🥘 Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp Coriander Leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice

📌 Procedure

  • Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add green chili and ginger-garlic paste, cooking until fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  • Cook until tomatoes turn soft and the mixture thickens.
  • Crack eggs into the pan and mix well.
  • Stir continuously and cook until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.
  • Sprinkle garam masala and coriander leaves, then mix well.
  • Drizzle lemon juice before serving.

Enjoy your spicy and flavorful Egg Bhurji with toast or roti! 🥚🔥
