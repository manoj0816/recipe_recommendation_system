Garlic Butter Chicken with Roasted Veggies🍗🥦

Garlic Butter Chicken with Roasted Veggies 🍗🥦

🥘 Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (sliced)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

📌 Procedure

  • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  • In a pan, heat 1 tbsp butter and sear the chicken on both sides for 2-3 minutes until golden brown.
  • In the same pan, add minced garlic and the remaining butter. Let it melt and coat the chicken.
  • Transfer the chicken to a baking tray.
  • In a bowl, mix the broccoli, cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, and carrots with olive oil, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  • Spread the veggies around the chicken on the tray.
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked and veggies are roasted.
  • Let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  • Serve hot with your favorite dip or rice.

🔥 Enjoy your delicious and healthy Garlic Butter Chicken with Roasted Veggies! 😋🍽️

Spicy Shrimp Tacos with Avocado Slaw🌮🦐

Spicy Shrimp Tacos with Avocado Slaw 🌮🦐

🥘 Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 8 small corn tortillas

🥑 Avocado Slaw

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage (purple or green)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 ripe avocado (mashed)
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt or sour cream
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

📌 Procedure

  • In a bowl, mix shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, salt, pepper, and lime juice. Let marinate for 10 minutes.
  • In another bowl, combine shredded cabbage, carrots, mashed avocado, Greek yogurt, lime juice, cilantro, salt, and pepper. Mix well to make the slaw.
  • Heat a pan over medium-high heat and cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and fully cooked.
  • Warm the corn tortillas on a dry skillet or directly over a flame for a few seconds on each side.
  • Assemble the tacos by adding avocado slaw to each tortilla and topping with spicy shrimp.
  • Garnish with extra lime wedges and fresh cilantro.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!

🔥 Enjoy your flavorful Spicy Shrimp Tacos with Avocado Slaw! 🌮😋

Creamy Mushroom Pasta

Creamy Mushroom Pasta 🍝🍄

🥘 Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (spaghetti, fettuccine, or penne)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 200g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large pan, heat butter and olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 5-7 minutes until they release moisture and turn golden brown.
  • Season with salt, black pepper, and red chili flakes (if using).
  • Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  • Lower the heat and add the heavy cream, stirring well.
  • Mix in the grated Parmesan cheese and dried oregano. Stir until the sauce thickens.
  • Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until well coated.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan if desired.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Creamy Mushroom Pasta!

🔥 Enjoy your rich and creamy mushroom pasta! 🍝😋

Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon🍋🐟

Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon 🍋🐟

🥘 Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp lemon zest
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • Lemon slices (for serving)

📌 Procedure

  • In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, black pepper, and red chili flakes.
  • Place the salmon fillets in a dish and pour the marinade over them. Let them marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  • Preheat the grill or a grill pan over medium-high heat.
  • Brush the grill with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  • Place the salmon fillets skin-side down and grill for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  • Remove from the grill and let rest for a minute.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon slices.
  • Enjoy with roasted vegetables, rice, or a fresh salad!

🔥 Enjoy your flavorful Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon! 🍽️😋

Beef Stroganoff with Egg Noodles🥩🍜

Beef Stroganoff with Egg Noodles 🥩🍜

🥘 Ingredients

  • 250g egg noodles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g beef sirloin or tenderloin (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 200g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Cook the egg noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • Heat 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  • Season the beef strips with salt, pepper, and paprika, then sear them in the pan for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add the remaining butter and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
  • Add the sliced mushrooms and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  • Pour in the beef broth, Worcestershire sauce, and Dijon mustard. Stir well and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Reduce the heat and stir in the sour cream until smooth.
  • Return the cooked beef to the pan and mix well, letting it heat through for 2 minutes.
  • Serve the beef stroganoff over egg noodles and garnish with fresh parsley.
  • Enjoy your creamy and savory Beef Stroganoff!

🔥 Enjoy your delicious Beef Stroganoff with Egg Noodles! 🍽️😋

Paneer Butter Masala with Naan🧈🧀🥖

Paneer Butter Masala with Naan 🧈🧀🥖

🥘 Ingredients

For Paneer Butter Masala:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1/4 cup cashews (soaked and blended into a paste)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tbsp dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi, crushed)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

For Naan:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp butter (for brushing)
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic (for garlic naan, optional)

📌 Procedure

Paneer Butter Masala:

  • Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  • Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate.
  • Add turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  • Add the cashew paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Pour in water and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Add paneer cubes, sugar, and fresh cream. Stir gently and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Crush and sprinkle dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) over the curry.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and turn off the heat.

Naan:

  • In a bowl, mix flour, salt, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  • Add yogurt, warm milk, and oil. Knead into a soft dough.
  • Cover and let it rest for 1 hour.
  • Divide the dough into small balls and roll into oval shapes.
  • Sprinkle chopped garlic on top if making garlic naan.
  • Cook on a hot pan for 1-2 minutes on each side until bubbles appear.
  • Flip and cook directly over the flame or in a tandoor for a charred effect.
  • Brush with melted butter and serve hot.

🔥 Enjoy your rich and creamy Paneer Butter Masala with soft, buttery Naan! 🍽️😋

Classic Chicken Alfredo Pasta🍗🍝

Classic Chicken Alfredo Pasta 🍗🍝

🥘 Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Alfredo Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp red chili flakes (optional)

For the Pasta:

  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • Water (for boiling)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (for boiling water)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped, for garnish)

📌 Procedure

Cooking the Chicken:

  • Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  • Cook the chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until golden brown and fully cooked.
  • Remove from the pan, let it rest for a few minutes, then slice into strips.

Preparing the Pasta:

  • Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  • Cook fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  • Drain and set aside.

Making the Alfredo Sauce:

  • In the same pan, melt butter over medium heat.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Pour in heavy cream and whole milk, stirring continuously.
  • Add Parmesan cheese, salt, black pepper, Italian seasoning, and red chili flakes (if using).
  • Stir well until the sauce thickens (about 3-4 minutes).

Combining Everything:

  • Add the cooked pasta to the Alfredo sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  • Top with sliced chicken and mix gently.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan if desired.
  • Serve hot and enjoy!

🔥 Enjoy your creamy and delicious Chicken Alfredo Pasta! 🍽️😋

Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu🥦🥕🍲

Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu 🥦🥕🍲

🥘 Ingredients

For the Tofu:

  • 200g firm tofu (cubed)
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Stir-Fry Vegetables:

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (or olive oil)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced, any color)
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup carrots (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)

For the Stir-Fry Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tsp sriracha or chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (mixed with 2 tbsp water)

For Garnish:

  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander or green onions (chopped)

📌 Procedure

Preparing the Tofu:

  • Pat dry the tofu and cut it into cubes.
  • In a bowl, mix tofu with cornstarch, salt, black pepper, and soy sauce.
  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Fry tofu cubes for 5-6 minutes, flipping until golden brown and crispy. Set aside.

Making the Stir-Fry:

  • In the same pan, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat.
  • Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Add onions, bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, and snap peas.
  • Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.

Preparing the Sauce:

  • In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and sriracha.
  • Add the cornstarch slurry and mix well.
  • Pour the sauce into the stir-fried vegetables and mix.

Combining Everything:

  • Add the crispy tofu back into the pan and toss everything well.
  • Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  • Garnish with sesame seeds and fresh coriander or green onions.
  • Serve hot with rice or noodles.

🔥 Enjoy your flavorful and healthy Vegetable Stir-Fry with Tofu! 🍽️😋

Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa🫑🍛

Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa 🫑🍛

🥘 Ingredients

For the Bell Peppers:

  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)

For the Quinoa Filling:

  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed and drained)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth (or water)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup tomatoes (diced)
  • 1/2 cup black beans (cooked or canned, drained)
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or mozzarella, optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish)

📌 Procedure

Preparing the Bell Peppers:

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cut the tops off the bell peppers and remove the seeds and membranes.
  • Brush the inside and outside of the peppers with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  • Place them in a baking dish, cut side up, and bake for 10 minutes to soften slightly.

Cooking the Quinoa:

  • In a saucepan, bring vegetable broth or water to a boil.
  • Add the rinsed quinoa, cover, and reduce heat to low.
  • Simmer for about 15 minutes, or until the quinoa is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  • Fluff with a fork and set aside.

Making the Filling:

  • In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until soft.
  • Add garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  • Stir in diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and cooked quinoa.
  • Add cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
  • Cook for 3-4 minutes until everything is well combined.

Stuffing and Baking:

  • Spoon the quinoa mixture into the pre-baked bell peppers.
  • Sprinkle shredded cheese on top if using.
  • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 15 minutes.
  • Remove the foil and bake for another 5 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Serving:

  • Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  • Serve warm and enjoy!

🔥 Enjoy your delicious and healthy Stuffed Bell Peppers with Quinoa! 🍽️😋

Homemade Sushi Rolls🍣

Homemade Sushi Rolls 🍣

🥘 Ingredients

For the Sushi Rice:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

For the Sushi Rolls:

  • 5-6 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 small cucumber (julienned)
  • 1 small carrot (julienned)
  • 1 ripe avocado (sliced)
  • 200g fresh salmon or imitation crab (optional, sliced thinly)
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (for garnish)

For Serving:

  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

📌 Procedure

Preparing the Sushi Rice:

  • Rinse sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • Cook rice with 2 1/2 cups water in a rice cooker or on the stovetop.
  • In a small pan, heat rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved (do not boil).
  • Once rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and mix in the vinegar mixture.
  • Let the rice cool to room temperature.

Assembling the Sushi Rolls:

  • Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat (shiny side down).
  • Wet your hands and spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving 1 inch uncovered at the top.
  • Lay cucumber, carrot, avocado, and your choice of fish or crab in a line near the bottom edge of the rice.

Rolling the Sushi:

  • Using the sushi mat, roll the nori tightly over the filling.
  • Continue rolling while applying gentle pressure.
  • Seal the edge with a little water.
  • Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into even pieces.

Serving:

  • Garnish with sesame seeds.
  • Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  • Enjoy your homemade sushi rolls! 🍣😋

🔥 Enjoy your fresh and delicious Homemade Sushi Rolls! 🍽️

Shepherd’s Pie 🥧🥔

Shepherd’s Pie 🥧🥔

🥘 Ingredients

For the Meat Filling:

  • 500g ground lamb (or beef for Cottage Pie)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup carrots (diced)
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme (or 1/2 tsp dried thyme)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

For the Mashed Potato Topping:

  • 3 large potatoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

📌 Procedure

Preparing the Meat Filling:

  • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and garlic, sauté until soft.
  • Add ground lamb (or beef) and cook until browned.
  • Stir in carrots, peas, and corn, and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Add tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
  • Pour in beef broth and let simmer for 10 minutes until thickened.

Making the Mashed Potato Topping:

  • Boil potatoes in salted water for 15-20 minutes until fork-tender.
  • Drain and mash with butter, milk, salt, and black pepper.
  • Mix in shredded cheese for extra creaminess (optional).

Assembling and Baking:

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Spread the meat mixture evenly in a baking dish.
  • Top with mashed potatoes, spreading evenly with a spoon or spatula.
  • Use a fork to create texture on top.
  • Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Serving:

  • Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley (optional).
  • Enjoy your delicious homemade Shepherd’s Pie! 😋

🔥 Enjoy your comforting Shepherd’s Pie! 🍽️

Mediterranean Chickpea Salad with Grilled Chicken🥗🍗

Mediterranean Chickpea Salad with Grilled Chicken 🥗🍗

🥘 Ingredients

For the Grilled Chicken:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

For the Chickpea Salad:

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/2 cucumber (diced)
  • 1/4 red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped)

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

📌 Procedure

Grilling the Chicken:

  • Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium heat.
  • In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • Rub the mixture over the chicken breasts.
  • Grill the chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  • Remove from heat, let rest for a few minutes, then slice.

Preparing the Chickpea Salad:

  • In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, olives, and feta cheese.
  • Add chopped parsley for extra freshness.

Making the Dressing:

  • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, oregano, salt, and black pepper.

Assembling:

  • Pour the dressing over the chickpea salad and toss well to combine.
  • Serve the salad in a bowl and top with sliced grilled chicken.

Serving:

  • Garnish with extra feta cheese or parsley if desired.
  • Enjoy your fresh and healthy Mediterranean Chickpea Salad with Grilled Chicken! 😋

🔥 Enjoy your nutritious and flavorful Mediterranean Chickpea Salad with Grilled Chicken! 🍽️🥗

Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice🍛🌿

Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice 🍛🌿

🥘 Ingredients

For the Green Curry:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp Thai green curry paste
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 small zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 cup baby eggplant (sliced, optional)
  • 1/2 cup green beans
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, tofu, or shrimp (optional)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (or soy sauce for vegetarian option)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp lime juice
  • 1/2 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 1-2 Thai green chilies (optional, for extra spice)

For the Jasmine Rice:

  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt

📌 Procedure

Cooking the Jasmine Rice:

  • Rinse jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  • Add the rinsed rice and salt, then reduce heat to low.
  • Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

Preparing the Thai Green Curry:

  • Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  • Add onions, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  • Stir in the green curry paste and cook for 1-2 minutes to release its flavors.
  • Pour in coconut milk and broth, stirring to combine.
  • Add bell peppers, zucchini, eggplant, and green beans. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Add cooked chicken, tofu, or shrimp (if using) and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  • Season with fish sauce (or soy sauce), brown sugar, and lime juice.
  • Stir in Thai basil leaves and Thai green chilies for extra spice.

Serving:

  • Serve the Thai Green Curry hot with fluffy jasmine rice.
  • Garnish with extra Thai basil or lime wedges.
  • Enjoy your aromatic and flavorful Thai Green Curry with Jasmine Rice! 😋

🔥 Experience the rich and authentic taste of Thailand with this homemade Thai Green Curry! 🍛🌿

Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon🍯🐟

Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon 🍯🐟

🥘 Ingredients

For the Salmon:

  • 4 salmon fillets (skin-on or skinless)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika (optional, for extra flavor)

For the Honey Garlic Glaze:

  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for spice)
  • 1 tbsp butter

📌 Procedure

Cooking the Salmon:

  • Pat dry the salmon fillets and season both sides with salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  • Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  • Place the salmon fillets skin-side down (if using skin-on) and cook for 4-5 minutes until crispy.
  • Flip and cook the other side for 3-4 minutes until golden brown.
  • Remove the salmon from the pan and set aside.

Preparing the Honey Garlic Glaze:

  • In the same pan, reduce the heat to medium and melt the butter.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Stir in honey, soy sauce, lemon juice, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  • Simmer the sauce for 1-2 minutes until slightly thickened.

Glazing the Salmon:

  • Return the cooked salmon fillets to the pan.
  • Spoon the glaze over the salmon and let it coat evenly.
  • Cook for another 1-2 minutes, basting with the glaze.

Serving:

  • Serve hot with steamed rice, roasted vegetables, or a fresh salad.
  • Garnish with chopped parsley or sesame seeds (optional).
  • Enjoy your delicious Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon! 😋

🔥 A perfect balance of sweet, savory, and garlicky flavors in every bite! 🍯🐟

Chicken Tikka Masala with Basmati Rice

Chicken Tikka Masala with Basmati Rice 🍛🔥

🥘 Ingredients

For the Chicken Tikka:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil

For the Masala Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 cup tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (or coconut milk for dairy-free)
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro (chopped, for garnish)

For the Basmati Rice:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (optional, for extra flavor)

📌 Procedure

Marinating the Chicken:

  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, garlic paste, ginger paste, garam masala, turmeric, cumin, paprika, red chili powder, salt, and oil.
  • Add chicken pieces, coat well, and marinate for at least 1 hour (overnight for best flavor).

Cooking the Chicken:

  • Heat a pan or grill over medium-high heat.
  • Cook the marinated chicken pieces for about 3-4 minutes per side until slightly charred and cooked through.
  • Remove from heat and set aside.

Making the Masala Sauce:

  • Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Stir in garlic and ginger paste, cooking for 30 seconds.
  • Add garam masala, cumin, turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Stir well.
  • Pour in the tomato purée and simmer for 5-7 minutes until thickened.
  • Stir in the cooked chicken pieces and mix well.
  • Add heavy cream (or coconut milk) and sugar, then simmer for another 5 minutes.
  • Adjust seasoning and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Cooking the Basmati Rice:

  • Rinse basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  • Add rice, salt, and cumin seeds (if using).
  • Cover and simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and let it sit for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

Serving:

  • Serve the Chicken Tikka Masala hot over fluffy basmati rice.
  • Garnish with extra cilantro and a drizzle of cream.
  • Enjoy your rich and flavorful Chicken Tikka Masala with Basmati Rice! 😋
