Grilled Chicken Salad 🥗🍗

Grilled Chicken Salad 🥗🍗

🥘 Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup red onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinaigrette dressing

📌 Procedure

  • Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  • Rub the chicken breasts with olive oil, lemon juice, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
  • Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  • Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  • In a large bowl, combine salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  • Top with sliced grilled chicken and crumbled feta cheese.
  • Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing and toss gently.
  • Serve fresh and enjoy your healthy Grilled Chicken Salad! 🥗🔥

Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice

Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice 🍚🥦

🥘 Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (sliced, any color)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • 2 tbsp green onions (chopped, for garnish)

📌 Procedure

  • Heat olive oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  • Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Add broccoli, bell peppers, carrots, snap peas, and mushrooms. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.
  • Pour in soy sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, and red chili flakes (if using). Stir well.
  • Add the cooked rice and mix everything together.
  • Cook for another 2 minutes until everything is well combined.
  • Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  • Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice! 🍛🔥

Paneer Butter Masala with Naan 🧈🫓

Paneer Butter Masala with Naan 🧈🫓

🥘 Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 4 naan (store-bought or homemade)

📌 Procedure

  • Heat butter and oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  • Pour in the tomato puree and cook for 5-6 minutes until oil separates.
  • Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and coriander powder. Mix well.
  • Stir in tomato ketchup and kasuri methi.
  • Add milk and fresh cream, stirring continuously to form a rich gravy.
  • Gently add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and remove from heat.
  • Serve hot with soft naan for a delicious meal! 🧈🔥

Fish Curry with Rice 🐟🍛

Fish Curry with Rice 🐟🍛

🥘 Ingredients

  • 500g fish (any firm fish like kingfish, salmon, or tilapia, cut into pieces)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 1-2 green chilies (slit)
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • 2 cups cooked rice (for serving)

📌 Procedure

  • Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Add tomato puree and cook for 5-6 minutes until oil separates.
  • Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala.
  • Pour in coconut milk and tamarind pulp, stirring well.
  • Gently add fish pieces and cook for 8-10 minutes until fully cooked.
  • Adjust salt and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice for a flavorful meal! 🍛🔥

Pasta Alfredo 🍝🧀

🥘 Ingredients

  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1 tbsp parsley (chopped, for garnish)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken or mushrooms (optional, for variation)

📌 Procedure

  • Boil fettuccine pasta in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • In a pan, melt butter over medium heat and sauté minced garlic for 30 seconds.
  • Pour in heavy cream and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  • Add grated parmesan cheese, black pepper, salt, and nutmeg (if using), stirring continuously until smooth.
  • Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  • If using, add cooked chicken or mushrooms and mix well.
  • Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot. Enjoy your creamy Pasta Alfredo! 🍝🔥

Egg Fried Rice🍳🍚

Egg Fried Rice 🍳🍚

🥘 Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional, for spice)
  • 2 tbsp green onions (chopped, for garnish)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (for flavor, optional)

📌 Procedure

  • Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  • Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until fully cooked. Remove and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add the remaining oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  • Add chopped carrots, bell peppers, and green peas. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add the cooked rice and mix well.
  • Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), black pepper, salt, and red chili flakes. Stir to combine.
  • Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and mix everything together.
  • Drizzle sesame oil for extra flavor (optional).
  • Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious Egg Fried Rice! 🍳🔥

Chole Bhature

Chole Bhature 🍛🥯

🥘 Ingredients

For Chole (Chickpea Curry):

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped, for garnish)

For Bhature (Fried Bread):

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil (for deep frying)

📌 Procedure

For Chole:

  • Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with water and a little salt for 4-5 whistles or until soft.
  • Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  • Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  • Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Cook until oil separates.
  • Add the boiled chickpeas along with some water and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Stir in amchur powder and adjust salt as needed.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

For Bhature:

  • In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, baking soda, salt, sugar, and yogurt.
  • Add water as needed and knead into a soft dough.
  • Cover the dough and let it rest for 2 hours.
  • Divide the dough into equal portions and roll out into medium-sized discs.
  • Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry the bhature until golden brown.
  • Drain excess oil and serve hot with chole.

Enjoy your crispy Bhature with spicy Chole! 🍛🔥

Mutton Biryani🍖🍚

Mutton Biryani 🍖🍚

🥘 Ingredients

For Marination:

  • 500g mutton (bone-in, cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt (to taste)

For Biryani Masala:

  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup milk (with saffron strands soaked)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fried onions (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Marinating the Mutton

  • In a bowl, mix mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice.
  • Cover and marinate for at least 2 hours (or overnight for better flavor).

Step 2: Cooking the Rice

  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • In a pot, boil water with bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, and salt.
  • Add the soaked rice and cook until it's 70% done. Drain and set aside.

Step 3: Preparing the Biryani Masala

  • Heat oil and ghee in a deep pan.
  • Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook until fragrant.
  • Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt.
  • Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  • Add marinated mutton and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  • Add 1/2 cup water, cover, and cook on low heat until the mutton is tender.

Step 4: Layering and Dum Cooking

  • In a large pot, layer half of the cooked rice over the mutton masala.
  • Sprinkle chopped mint, coriander, saffron milk, and fried onions.
  • Add the remaining rice and repeat the garnish.
  • Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat (Dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  • Remove from heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your aromatic and flavorful Mutton Biryani with raita! 🍖🔥

Paneer Tikka Wrap🌯🧀

Paneer Tikka Wrap 🌯🧀

🥘 Ingredients

For Paneer Tikka:

  • 200g paneer (cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp gram flour (besan, for binding)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp oil

For Wrap:

  • 4 whole wheat rotis/tortillas
  • 1/2 cup sliced onions
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 2 tbsp green chutney
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp butter (for toasting)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Marinating the Paneer

  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, chaat masala, lemon juice, gram flour, and salt.
  • Add paneer cubes and coat them well in the marinade.
  • Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Cooking the Paneer Tikka

  • Heat oil in a pan and sauté marinated paneer until golden brown on all sides.
  • Add sliced onions and bell peppers to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • Sprinkle chaat masala and mix well.

Step 3: Assembling the Wrap

  • Spread green chutney and mayonnaise (if using) on a roti/tortilla.
  • Place the cooked paneer tikka mixture in the center.
  • Add some sliced onions and bell peppers for crunch.
  • Roll the wrap tightly and toast it lightly on a pan with butter.

Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Paneer Tikka Wrap! 🌯🔥

Dal Tadka with Jeera Rice🍛🍚

Dal Tadka with Jeera Rice 🍛🍚

🥘 Ingredients

For Dal Tadka:

  • 1 cup toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander (chopped, for garnish)

For Jeera Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 3.5 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Cooking the Dal

  • Wash toor dal thoroughly and soak for 30 minutes.
  • Pressure cook dal with 3 cups of water, turmeric, and salt for 3-4 whistles.
  • Mash the cooked dal slightly and set aside.

Step 2: Preparing the Dal Tadka

  • Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and dried red chilies.
  • Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook until fragrant.
  • Add tomatoes, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala.
  • Cook until the tomatoes turn mushy and oil separates.
  • Mix in the cooked dal and simmer for 5 minutes.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander and set aside.

Step 3: Preparing Jeera Rice

  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  • Heat ghee in a pan and add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cardamom.
  • Once fragrant, add the soaked rice and sauté for a minute.
  • Add water and salt, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer until rice is cooked.

Serve hot Dal Tadka with fragrant Jeera Rice and enjoy! 🍛🔥

Shrimp Tacos🌮🦐

Shrimp Tacos 🌮🦐

🥘 Ingredients

For the Shrimp:

  • 250g shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

For the Slaw:

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage (purple or green)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise or Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp honey
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

For the Tacos:

  • 6 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup diced avocado
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta or cotija cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1 tbsp sour cream (optional)
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional)
  • Lime wedges (for serving)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Cooking the Shrimp

  • In a bowl, mix shrimp with olive oil, paprika, cumin, garlic powder, chili powder, salt, and lime juice.
  • Heat a pan over medium-high heat and cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until opaque and slightly charred.

Step 2: Preparing the Slaw

  • In a mixing bowl, combine shredded cabbage, carrots, and cilantro.
  • Add mayonnaise (or Greek yogurt), lime juice, honey, salt, and pepper.
  • Mix well and set aside.

Step 3: Assembling the Tacos

  • Warm the tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
  • Place a spoonful of slaw in each tortilla.
  • Add a few cooked shrimp on top.
  • Garnish with avocado, crumbled cheese, chopped onions, and sour cream.
  • Drizzle with hot sauce and serve with lime wedges.

Enjoy your delicious Shrimp Tacos with a squeeze of fresh lime! 🌮🔥

Masala Dosa with Sambar🥞🍛

Masala Dosa with Sambar 🥞🍛

🥘 Ingredients

For Dosa Batter:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1/4 cup poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water (as required)
  • Oil or ghee (for cooking)

For Potato Masala (Dosa Filling):

  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chana dal (Bengal gram)
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt (to taste)

For Sambar:

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (chopped)
  • 1 small carrot (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup tamarind water
  • 1 tbsp sambar powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Preparing the Dosa Batter

  • Wash and soak rice, urad dal, fenugreek seeds, and poha in water for 6-8 hours.
  • Grind the mixture into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  • Mix in salt and let the batter ferment overnight (8-12 hours).

Step 2: Preparing the Potato Masala

  • Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, and curry leaves.
  • Once mustard seeds splutter, add onions and green chilies, sauté until golden brown.
  • Add turmeric and mashed potatoes, mix well.
  • Season with salt and garnish with coriander leaves.

Step 3: Cooking the Sambar

  • Pressure cook toor dal with water and turmeric until soft. Mash and set aside.
  • In a pan, heat oil, add mustard seeds, dried red chilies, and curry leaves.
  • Add onions, tomatoes, carrots, and cook until soft.
  • Add tamarind water, sambar powder, salt, and cooked dal.
  • Simmer for 10 minutes, garnish with coriander leaves.

Step 4: Making the Masala Dosa

  • Heat a dosa pan and pour a ladleful of batter, spreading it in a circular motion.
  • Drizzle some oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  • Place a portion of the potato masala in the center.
  • Fold the dosa and serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney.

Enjoy your crispy Masala Dosa with hot Sambar! 🥞🔥

Veg Pulao with Raita 🍚🥒

Veg Pulao with Raita 🍚🥒

🥘 Ingredients

For Veg Pulao:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, bell peppers, etc.)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

For Raita:

  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 cucumber (grated or finely chopped)
  • 1/2 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp black salt (optional)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Cooking the Veg Pulao

  • Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • Heat ghee or oil in a pan and add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom.
  • Once fragrant, add sliced onions and green chili, sauté until golden brown.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell disappears.
  • Add mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
  • Mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
  • Add drained rice and sauté for a minute.
  • Pour in 2 cups of water, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer on low heat until rice is cooked.
  • Fluff the pulao with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Step 2: Preparing the Raita

  • In a bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth.
  • Add cucumber, onion, tomato, and coriander leaves.
  • Season with salt, black salt, and roasted cumin powder.
  • Mix well and refrigerate for 10 minutes before serving.

Serve the flavorful Veg Pulao with refreshing Raita and enjoy! 🍚🔥

Stuffed Paratha with Curd🫓🥣

Stuffed Paratha with Curd 🫓🥣

🥘 Ingredients

For the Paratha Dough:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee

For the Stuffing (Potato Filling):

  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)

For the Curd (Yogurt Side):

  • 1 cup fresh yogurt (curd)
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Preparing the Dough

  • In a bowl, mix whole wheat flour, salt, and oil.
  • Gradually add water and knead into a soft dough.
  • Cover and let it rest for 20 minutes.

Step 2: Preparing the Stuffing

  • In a mixing bowl, combine mashed potatoes, green chili, cumin powder, garam masala, chaat masala, red chili powder, salt, and coriander leaves.
  • Mix well to form a flavorful stuffing.

Step 3: Making the Stuffed Paratha

  • Divide the dough into equal portions and roll into small balls.
  • Take one dough ball, roll it into a small circle, and place a portion of the stuffing in the center.
  • Seal the edges by bringing the dough together and gently flatten it.
  • Roll it out again into a round paratha without applying too much pressure.
  • Heat a tawa (griddle) and place the rolled paratha on it.
  • Cook for 1-2 minutes, then flip and apply ghee or oil on top.
  • Cook both sides until golden brown and crispy.

Step 4: Preparing the Curd

  • In a bowl, whisk yogurt until smooth.
  • Add roasted cumin powder, salt, and chopped coriander leaves.
  • Mix well and serve chilled with paratha.

Enjoy your delicious Stuffed Paratha with cool and refreshing Curd! 🫓🔥

Chicken Kebab with Hummus

Chicken Kebab with Hummus 🍢🥣

🥘 Ingredients

For Chicken Kebab:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Wooden or metal skewers
  • Chopped coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Onion rings and lemon wedges (for serving)

For Hummus:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight & boiled)
  • 2 tbsp tahini (sesame paste)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tbsp water (if needed for consistency)
  • 1 tsp paprika (for garnish)

📌 Procedure

Step 1: Marinating the Chicken

  • In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, cumin, garam masala, coriander powder, salt, and oil.
  • Add the chicken cubes and coat well with the marinade.
  • Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor).

Step 2: Cooking the Chicken Kebab

  • Preheat the grill or pan over medium heat.
  • Thread the marinated chicken onto skewers.
  • Cook for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through.
  • Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with onion rings and lemon wedges.

Step 3: Preparing Hummus

  • In a blender, add boiled chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, cumin, and salt.
  • Blend until smooth, adding water if needed for a creamy texture.
  • Transfer to a bowl and drizzle with olive oil.
  • Sprinkle paprika on top for garnish.

Serve the juicy Chicken Kebab with creamy Hummus and enjoy! 🍢🔥
